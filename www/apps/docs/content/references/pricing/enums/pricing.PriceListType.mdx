# PriceListType

The price list's type.

## SALE

 **SALE** = `"sale"`

The price list's prices are used for a sale.

___

## OVERRIDE

 **OVERRIDE** = `"override"`

The price list's prices override original prices. This affects the calculated price of associated price sets.
