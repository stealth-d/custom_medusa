# ProductStatus

The status of a product.

## Enumeration Members

### DRAFT

 **DRAFT** = `"draft"`

The product is a draft. It's not viewable by customers.

### PROPOSED

 **PROPOSED** = `"proposed"`

The product is proposed, but not yet published.

### PUBLISHED

 **PUBLISHED** = `"published"`

The product is published.

### REJECTED

 **REJECTED** = `"rejected"`

The product is rejected. It's not viewable by customers.
